About the Game

HectoClash is a fast-paced multiplayer math puzzle game where players compete to reach the number 100 using six given digits and basic operators. You can play solo or challenge your friends!

Game Modes

  • 1v1 Online Multiplayer: Battle your friends or random players in real-time.
  • AI Mode: Practice against computer opponents of various difficulty levels.
  • Offline Mode: Play locally on the same device with a friend.
